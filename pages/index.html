Plug empowers individuals and small organizations with facebook pages to easily and efficiently engage with their audience via Messenger.



Now you and your team members can:

  • Save time - reply with a few clicks

    Quickly select and send pre-crafted messages to your customers.
  • Be on brand - with pre-crafted messages

    Respond to common questions or customer requests by sending pre-approved and on-brand responses.
  • Enable customers to self navigate

    You can setup and send Menus, Quick Replies and other responses with ease - these nifty Facebook tech features are typically used only by code ninjas.
    To use Plug you need to ask Facebook to grant us the following:
  • Access to your public profile and email address, to help us identify you every time you log in.
  • Permission to manage pages, this helps us identify the Facebook Pages you manage.
  • Permission to send messages on behalf of your pages, this would help us send messages on your behalf. (Don't worry, we will only send what you ask us to send!)
  • Permission to send messages from pages you manage anytime after First User Interaction, this would help us to send messages that that you want your customers to receive even when they haven't contacted you in the last 24 hours (again, Don't worry, we will only send what you ask us to send!).
  • You can always disable Plug in your Facebook Page settings.
    • When you click Login, Facebook will ask you to grant us the above permissions
